Naren Dasan

My interests are in Artifical Intellegence, Robotics, and Tangible Interaction Design mostly surrounding systems with multiple agents. I've done other work in the past on distributed and embedded system, the web and high performace. Right now I am working on the developement of Autonomous Vehicles at NVIDIA. I am a Colorado native and completed my undergrad at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in Computer Engineering. In my free time I work on open source, play rugby, soccer and tennis, race bikes and from 2016 to 2017 I served as the Chair of the UIUC Chapter of the ACM.
