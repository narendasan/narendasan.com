Naren Dasan

I am a student and researcher in Artifical Intellegence, Robotics, and Interaction Design with other work in Embedded Systems and Web Infrastructure. I am a Colorado native doing much of my early work there. I am currently completing my undergrad at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in Computer Engineering. In my free time I help organize Open Source Hackatons, play rugby, soccer and tennis, race bikes and from 2016 to 2017 I served as the Chair of the UIUC Chapter of the ACM.
