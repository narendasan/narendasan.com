The Homestead Strike: Instigator of Revolution, Reaction and Reform

Senior Division, Group Website
By: Naren Dasan and Jonah Weil


Thesis 
The Homestead Strike itself was a reaction to failed union negotiations. It incited attempts at revolution, caused public reaction and instigated reform in steel, labor, and politics.
